The Peppa Pig Family

Welcome to the fun world of Peppa Pig! Meet the Pig family and learn about each one.

Papa (Daddy) Pig

Daddy Pig is Peppa’s father. He loves pancakes, reading the newspaper, and driving the car. He works as an architect and thinks he’s an expert at everything!

Peppa Pig

Peppa is a cheerful little pig who loves jumping in muddy puddles, playing with friends, and spending time with her family.

George Pig

George is Peppa’s younger brother. He’s shy, loves dinosaurs, and often says “Dine-saw!”

Mummy Pig

Mummy Pig is kind, smart, and works from home. She enjoys cooking, gardening, and keeping the family organized.
